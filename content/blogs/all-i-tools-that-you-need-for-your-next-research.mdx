Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools have become valuable resources in modern research across various disciplines. These tools help researchers streamline complex tasks such as literature reviews, data collection, data analysis, and report writing. AI-powered systems can quickly process large volumes of information, identify trends and patterns, and generate insights that might be difficult to detect manually.

In academic and scientific research, AI tools support activities such as finding relevant publications, summarizing articles, organizing references, analyzing quantitative and qualitative data, and detecting anomalies in datasets. They also assist in improving research productivity by reducing repetitive tasks and enabling faster decision-making.

Popular AI research tools include ChatGPT, Elicit, Semantic Scholar, Scite, and Research Rabbit. By enhancing efficiency, accuracy, and knowledge discovery, AI tools are transforming the way researchers conduct and communicate their work.
